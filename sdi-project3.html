Project 3

My theme is my nightly free-time routine after the kids are in bed and once the house work and school work are completed. For this project, I decided to go more into detail about playing COD Black Ops 2, by treyarch, online if my clan is available.

In my project, I refer to the term "clan". For those who do not play video games on-line and are not familiar with the term, it refers to a group of people who play as a team in on-line matches. Also, I used the term gameMode to represent what kind of mode we will play. For example "Kill Confirmed" is a game mode in COD Black Ops 2 where you kill and enemy and then have to collect their dog tag to get the full points. "Capture the Flag" is a game mode where your goal as a team is to get the enemy flag and bring it back to your base while protecting the flag at your base.
